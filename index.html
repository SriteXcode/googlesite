International Symposium on Intelligent, Secure, and Deep Learning Systems (ISIDL-2025)

Date - 28th - 29th August 2025

Organised by - Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Delhi, India

About the Symposium

The International Symposium on Intelligent, Secure, and Deep Learning Systems (ISIDL 2025) is a premier global forum dedicated to the advancement of artificial intelligence, deep learning, and secure next generation systems. The symposium brings together leading researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and academicians to present their latest research findings, share innovative ideas, and discuss emerging trends in intelligent systems and trustworthy AI technologies.

Objectives and Scope

  • Deep learning architectures and optimization
  • Trustworthy, explainable, and robust AI
  • Secure and privacy-preserving machine learning
  • AI for cybersecurity and next generation networks
  • Intelligent decision-support systems for healthcare, industry, and smart cities
  • Bio-inspired and hybrid optimization techniques
  • Applications of intelligent systems in real-world environments

Plagiarism Policy

  1. The paper prior to submission should be checked for plagiarism from licensed plagiarism softwares like Turnitin/iAuthenticate etc. The similarity content should not exceed 15%.
  2. No self-plagiarism or plagiarism from others’ work should exist.
  3. If any model/figure/table/data/comment from previously published work is used, it must be properly cited.
  4. Language should not copy directly from the source material.
  5. If copyrighted material is used, prior permission must be acquired.
